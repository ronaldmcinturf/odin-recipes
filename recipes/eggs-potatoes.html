Eggs and Potots

The recipeeeeeee

Eating 4-5 red potots a day will make you a strong young buck.Combo that with 2-3 eggs and you ascend to super saiyan levels.

Carbs are not the enemy!

Ingredients

  • 4-5 whole potots
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Bbq sauce
  • 2-3 eggs

Instructions

  1. Chop potots
  2. Fry eggs on stove top
  3. Throw it in the airfyer. DONE.
