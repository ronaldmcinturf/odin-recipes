Oatmeal is underated! So so good for you.

Again... say it with me, carbs are not the enemy! One might say overnight oats are the overnight GOAT

Ingredients

  • 2 packets of quakers oats
  • your choice of milk
  • your choice of toppings

Instructions

  1. Put oats in a mason jar
  2. Pour in milk till just covers oats
  3. Throw it in the airfyer. DONE.
